About Khaniyara

Village Khaniyara is located at Dharamsala,Himachal pradesh.

It is a beautiful village surrounded by Pine trees,Large Mountains & Rivers.

The Village is blessed to have Lord Indru Nag Temple which is considered as The Lord of Rain.

Apart from this Khaniyara village Panchayat was once considered as the Richest Panchayat in Asia Continent.
